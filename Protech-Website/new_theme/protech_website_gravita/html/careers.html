Hadoop Developer
Overview We're looking for a creative, outgoing, open-minded individual with experience in Java and data processing systems such as Hadoop. The individual should be interested in new technologies and be comfortable with a constantly changing work environment.

Qualifications
  • BS/MS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • 5+ years strong experience in Hive, Pig, Oozie, Java and SQL
  • 10+ years of software development, architecture and design experience
  • Experience with open source data processing systems such as Hadoop.
  • Experience with relational databases and data warehouses.
  • Experience with a variety of other development environments such as Scala, Python, JavaScript, C++, Linux, Apache
  • Experience delivering big data solutions using major Hadoop distros and platforms like Cloudera, HortonWorks, MapR or Amazon EMR is essential. NoSQL databases like HBase, Cassandra and MongoDB a plus.
  • Experience delivering and operating SaaS product offerings in an iterative, agile software lifecycle
  • Should have good design skills in addition, be able to define code pieces, components required to implement a use-case and also be able to do quick evaluation of tools
  • Bonus points: Data Analytics, Telecoms and/or Marketing experience

Responsibilities
  • Research and develop methods to measure, forecast, and analyze revenue & performance.
  • Define big data storage and processing requirements and then to size and install Hadoop clusters if required.
  • Play a critical role in developing the broad plan for the business, working closely with senior executives to develop business targets and resource requirements. Help form long-term technical and business strategy and goals.
  • Play a key role in rethinking and building our future reporting and analytics infrastructure.
  • Participate in the complete life-cycle of the project utilizing agile development methodology.
  • Design and build fast analytics environment on large datasets/clusters
  • Design and develop solutions using Hadoop to tackle big data, information retrieval, and analytics problems.
  • Troubleshoot and develop code in Java, Python, and Ruby.
  • Create and update Hive schema to support a highly-available data warehouse for BI teams.
  • Manage creation, scheduling, and monitoring of ETL and other data processes.
