IdeaElectronic Content/Document Management is the process of managing documentation electronically through the conversion of existing and incoming documents to an electronic format, allowing users to view documentation electronically within their current system.

Process Protech has incorporated a document management process into our child support Case Management module making paper case files available to users through a simple easy to use screen. Authorized users have the ability to access electronic folders, filter contents of electronic folders, view electronic documents and initiate the attachment of a document to an electronic folder.



DesignUser Features Include:

  • Document Index – Link a single document to a case, a member, or both
  • Quick Note – Add a free form note that identifies the document
  • Document Alert – Send an alert regarding new or changed documents
  • Bulk Scan Option – Scan multiple documents
  • View Options – Look through multiple documents at once or view a larger image of just one
  • Document Tool Bar – Zoom, rotate and navigate through documents
